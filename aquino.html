About Bam Aquino  

Seeking to represent laborers and in turn, the masses in the senate, "Bam" champions the voice and rights of laborers. Centering his campaign around living wage, regularization, rights to unionism, self-organization, and collective action. With over 30 years of experience working towards labor rights and justice, Bam comes from the working sector and seeks to better life for all. Stand with workers and progressives for a labor win.
