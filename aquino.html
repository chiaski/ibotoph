About Bam Aquino  

A familiar name, incumbent (current) Senator Bam Aquino is once again in the running for re-election. A social entrepreneur and politician with 32 billes passed into law since 2013, he centers on "entrepreneurship, employment, and education". With a strong focus on the youth and small businesses, Bam was also previously Chairperson of the National Youth Commission and worked with social enterprise companies to elevate the national economy. With a history of youth-oriented, Philippine-centered policy and a vision to uplift the nation economically—Aquino is a reliable voice to count on.
